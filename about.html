About Me

Biography

London Rosemary was born in Las Vegas, and has lived in Salt Lake City Utah, Manassas Virginia and West Palm Beach Florida. She holds an International Baccalaureate diploma from G-Star Academy of the Arts with a background in Theater Arts. London attended the University of Utah, where she studied Stage Management. During her time at U of U she recognized shortcomings in the technology tools available to the arts, to serve their specific needs, and has transitioned to attend the Flatiron School Software Engineering program. She hopes to apply her knowledge to help develop technology solutions that better serve the unique needs of the arts. London is an avid volunteer, and has served hundreds of hours in the community, both with Habitat for Humanity and the Lake Worth Playhouse. In addition, she has served Habitat internationally, having participated in building affordable housing in India, and advocating for affordable housing in Washington D.C. London is the recipient of a student scholarship from Habitat for Humanity of Palm Beach County, as well as the recipient of the Flatiron School Women in Technology Scholarship.
